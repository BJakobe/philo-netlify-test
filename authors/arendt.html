Fabrication and Destruction
of Nature

Another issue that was brought up recently was the acquisition of dolomite sand to be used for the beautification of Manila Bay. The pulverized dolomite rock was sourced from Alcoy, Cebu as a substitute for sand and gravel. However, the acquisition of the sand was detrimental to the ecology of the surrounding mining site and the fine particles pose health-related issues. Moreover, marine scientists elaborated that the dolomite sand will not address the environmental issues of Manila Bay, implying that the utilization of it is pointless. Instead, they recommended the use of wastewater treatment plants to address the environmental problems of Manila Bay. The issue presented is the treatment towards natural resources only to result in futility, whereas other means and resources are dismissed.

The Need for the Artificial

The pandemic has forced the country to suspend gatherings in hopes to halt the spread of the disease. This meant that schools cannot hold on-site classes and continuation of it will require a remote form of learning. With the provision of online modules and classes, a student is required to be connected to the internet in some form of digital device. DepEd issued minimum specifications of these devices for online learning. Despite the optimistic outlook of the government on online classes, predicaments are bound to appear as it is being implemented for the first time. Accessibility is a concern as some households do not have means to acquire devices and maintain stable connections that are fundamental to online learning. Because of the current situation, Filipinos are now compelled to have the need for an artificial product that serves as a link to their other needs.

To Promise is to Expect

As the government imposed lockdown, the majority of Filipino households are left without a source of income. The government proposes a guarantee of subsidy to informal workers keeping them fed amidst the pandemic. In turn, this relieves them of the anxiety of obtaining a source of income since most of them are paid by the hour. Also, this makes poverty-stricken citizens hopeful of the government’s plans in mitigating the adverse effects of the pandemic, whereas lack of plans would provide insecurity among the families and uncertainty of the country’s direction in economic, social, political, and personal aspects.
